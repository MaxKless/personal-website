Building recommender systems: Funk's algorithm for simple matrix factorisation

I wrote this short paper at University as part of a lab on Statistical Learning Theory.
It starts by covering the mathematical foundation of Simon Funk's famously simplified approach to matrix factorization. In the later sections, I describe the provided dataset and additional regularization and parameter tweaking steps taken to optimize the effectiveness of the system. Finally, I analyze the results and put them into context with other attempted approaches.
