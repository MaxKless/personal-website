Intershop PWA

The Intershop PWA is an Angular-based progressive web app storefront for the Intershop Commerce Suite.
Developing the Intershop PWA has given me a broad insight into the process of building an enterprise-scale Angular project. I have gathered a lot of experience in writing angular components, services and tests in addition to working with a multitude of additional technologies and frameworks that are used within the PWA. For more information on my responsibilites, have a look at the detailed overview.
